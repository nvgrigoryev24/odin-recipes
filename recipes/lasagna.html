Lasanga

Ingredients:
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (24 oz) jar marinara sauce
- 12 lasagna noodles
- 15 oz ricotta cheese
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 egg
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain.
3. In a skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
4. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 15 minutes more.
9. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
